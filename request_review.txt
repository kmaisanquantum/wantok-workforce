I have updated `backend/src/admin/controllers/admin_controller.js` to address the Service Providers list segregation issue.

Changes:
1. Updated `getDashboardMetrics` SQL fallback to count users who have the 'provider' role OR have an entry in `provider_profiles`.
2. Updated `getAllUsers` provider filter branch to include users who have the 'provider' role OR have an entry in `provider_profiles`.

Both updates use the exact logic requested:
`EXISTS (SELECT 1 FROM provider_profiles p WHERE p.user_id = u.id)`

This ensures that the list view matches the dashboard count and excludes "pure customers" who were previously dumping into the provider list due to unsegregated generic flags.
